The Experience

THE.EX is not a conference. It is a curated, faith-driven experience designed to activate purpose, not just inspire it.

Two distinct yet connected journeys run simultaneously — GAL.EX for women and MAN.EX for men. Each is limited to 25 selected individuals, for a total of 50 people. You don’t register. You apply, and then you’re chosen. This is a weekend built for depth, connection, and real transformation — not surface-level inspiration.

Who This Is For
This experience was designed for a specific kind of person.

THE.EX is not for everyone — and that’s intentional. It’s for the ones who are done with surface-level events and ready for something that actually shifts the way they live. If any of the following resonate, this may be your moment.

✦You’re searching for clarity on your identity, calling, or next chapter — and you’re tired of figuring it out alone.
✦You want deeper alignment between your faith, your relationships, and the life you’re building — not just motivation, but real structure.
✦You’re ready for honest, guided conversations about healing, growth, and purpose — in a room small enough to be seen.
✦You value intentional community over large crowds — and you’re looking for people who are walking the same road.
✦You’re a leader, a builder, or someone becoming one — and you know growth requires the right environment.
✦You believe purpose is faith-driven, not self-made — and you’re ready to step into it with people who believe the same.

Questions
Frequently Asked
What is THE.EX?
THE.EX is a curated, faith-driven experience — not a traditional conference. It brings together 50 people total for two days of intimate sessions, guided conversations, and intentional community. The goal is transformation, not information.
Who is THE.EX for?
It’s for men and women who are serious about growth, purpose, and alignment. If you’re looking for clarity, healing, deeper faith, or meaningful community — and you’re willing to do the real work — this was designed for you.
Where is the event taking place?
THE.EX takes place in Westchester County, New York on June 26–27, 2026. The exact venue address is shared with selected attendees after confirmation.
What is the difference between GAL.EX and MAN.EX?
GAL.EX is the women’s experience, focused on identity, connection, and purpose. MAN.EX is the men’s experience, focused on alignment, leadership, and manhood. Both run simultaneously and come together for shared moments.
How does selection work?
You submit a short application. Our team reviews every one individually, looking for alignment, readiness, and genuine intention. Not everyone will be selected — this is by design to preserve the depth and intimacy of the experience.
What happens after I apply?
If selected, you’ll receive a confirmation with full event details, payment instructions, hotel booking information, and available add-on experiences. All details are shared after selection, not before.
Are lodging and add-ons included?
Lodging and meals are not included in the base experience but are available as optional add-ons. We’ve partnered with the Hotel of MTK for attendee accommodations. Details and pricing for all add-ons are shared after you’re selected.
What should I expect from the atmosphere?
Intimate, warm, and intentional. This is a room of 25 — not 250. Expect real conversations, guided sessions rooted in faith, moments of reflection, and a community that sees you. The environment is designed to feel safe, elevated, and transformative.
